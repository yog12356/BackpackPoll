Backpacks in School

There is much controversy over having backpacks being carried around from class to class. Some might say it is and security issue. Others might say it is madatory because of size of lockers and how much stuff we have. So we decided to do a public opinon poll, asking the student body wether or not students should be able to carry backpacks from class to class.

Analyze

As you can see the majority of the student body out of the 100 people at random we chose, said that students should be allowed to carry backpacks from class to class. 62% said they strongly agree, 16% said they somewhat they agree, 6% percent remained neutral. 10% Somewhat disagreed, while the last 6% person chose the strongly disagree.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the majority of people chose that students should be able to carry backpacks from class to class. That does not mean that it will be implemented, but that is what the students want.
